About ThreadCycle

Our mission to transform fashion

Our Mission

ThreadCycle exists to revolutionize how we think about fashion. We believe that style doesn't have to come at the cost of our planet. Through education, advocacy, and community action, we're building a world where fashion is circular, sustainable, and accessible to all.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every piece of clothing is valued, where waste is eliminated through thoughtful design and conscious consumption, and where the fashion industry operates in harmony with our environment and communities.

What We Do

  • Educate consumers about sustainable fashion practices
  • Connect people with ethical fashion brands and resources
  • Promote clothing repair, upcycling, and circular fashion
  • Advocate for transparency and accountability in the fashion industry
  • Build communities around sustainable fashion practices
