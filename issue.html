The Fashion Crisis

Understanding the environmental and social impact

The Scale of the Problem

The fashion industry is one of the world's most polluting industries, second only to oil. Fast fashion has created a culture of disposable clothing that's devastating our planet and exploiting workers worldwide.

The Human Cost

Beyond environmental damage, fast fashion perpetuates poor working conditions, unfair wages, and unsafe factories. Millions of garment workers, predominantly women, face exploitation to produce cheap clothing for global markets.
