January 15, 2025 News

New EU Regulations Target Fast Fashion Waste

The European Union has announced groundbreaking legislation requiring fashion brands to take responsibility for textile waste. Starting in 2026, all clothing manufacturers will need to implement take-back programs and ensure at least 30% of materials are recycled or sustainably sourced.

This marks a significant shift in how the fashion industry operates, with major brands already announcing plans to comply ahead of the deadline. Environmental groups are calling this a "watershed moment" for sustainable fashion.

December 28, 2024 Research

Study Reveals True Cost of Synthetic Fabrics

A new study from MIT reveals that synthetic fabrics release up to 700,000 microplastic fibers per wash cycle. These tiny particles end up in our oceans, affecting marine life and entering the food chain. The research emphasizes the importance of choosing natural fibers and using microfiber-catching laundry bags.
